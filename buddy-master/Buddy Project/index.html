Welcome to the Buddy project

Bullying is a situation where a person, called a bully, verbally or physically threatens or assaults a person, causing the person to feel a real or perceived power imbalance. Bullying can cause a person to feel extreme distress. It may lead to changes in sleep and dietary habits. It can even put someone at greater risk for committing suicide. This is why it's so important to prevent bullying.
